Job Title: Junior Full Stack Developer- Part Time
Company: Zehenify
Location: Remote
About Zehenify:
Zehenify is a cutting-edge SaaS platform dedicated to revolutionizing mental health services in
Pakistan. Our mission is to empower mental health professionals and individuals by providing
innovative tools for therapy management, client progress tracking, and personalized
assessments. At Zehenify, we foster a collaborative environment that values creativity, open
communication, and integrity. Join our passionate team as we work to break the stigma
surrounding mental health and make a lasting impact in the community.
Role Description:
We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Full Stack Developer to join the Zehenify
team. In this role, you will help build and maintain our next-generation mental health platform,
ensuring a seamless user experience across the frontend and backend. This is an excellent
opportunity to grow your skills in a dynamic, purpose-driven environment
Responsibilities:
● Develop features & maintain functionality, ensuring a seamless user experience across
both the frontend and backend.
● Build responsive and intuitive user interfaces using Next.js and React.js.
● Implement server-side rendering (SSR) and static site generation (SSG) to optimize
performance.
● Design and integrate backend systems using Node.js and Express.js.
● Develop and consume RESTful APIs to support various platform features.
● Manage and optimize databases with MongoDB, including schema design and
aggregation pipelines.
● Collaborate with the team to deliver high-quality, maintainable code.
● Troubleshoot and debug application issues to ensure reliability and scalability.
● Follow Git version control workflows to manage codebases effectively
Requirements:
Frontend Development:
- Expertise in Next.js and React.js fundamentals.
- Strong knowledge of HTML, CSS, & JavaScript (ES6+)
- Familiarity with server-side rendering (SSR) and static site generation (SSG)
- Experience with state management tools (e.g., Redux, Context API, or alternatives)
- Proficiency in responsive design and modern CSS frameworks (e.g., TailwindCSS,
Bootstrap, or Material-UI).
Backend Development:
- Strong experience with Node.js and Express.js for API development.
- Proficiency in designing and consuming RESTful APIs (GraphQL knowledge is a plus)
- Familiarity with authentication mechanisms like JWT, OAuth, or Passport.js.
- Experience with WebSockets for real-time communication (optional but valuable)
Database Management:
- Deep understanding of MongoDB, including schema design, indexing,& aggregation pipelines.
- Knowledge of MongoDB Atlas for cloud-based database management.
- Experience with database optimization and scaling.
Qualifications:
3rd and 4th-year undergraduate students or fresh graduates in Computer Science, Software
Engineering, or related fields are eligible to apply.
